List of parameters usable in harvesting goals:

croptype (Minecraft ID of a block ('wheat')):
Type of plant to harvest.

harvestitem (item, chance and (optional) required tag ('leather,50' or 'boudin,50,oven')):
Item to be harvested, with chance.

irrigationbonuscrop (item (from itemlist.txt)):
Boons for irrigated villages.

harvestblockstate (a Minecraft blockstate ('red_flower;type=blue_orchid')):
Blockstate the crop must have to be harvested. If not set, must have a meta of 7.
